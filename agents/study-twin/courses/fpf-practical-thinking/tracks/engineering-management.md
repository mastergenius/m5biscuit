# Engineering Management Track

The track is built around moves that improve real work:

1. Find the signal that triggered the work.
2. Bound the work honestly.
3. Define done as observable change.
4. Keep metrics in their role.
5. Hold rival explanations during incidents.
6. Record decisions with invariants and checks.
7. Communicate updates that reduce uncertainty.

## Competency Targets

`fpf.competency.frame-work`
: The learner can state signal, scope, out-of-scope, done, and risk for a current task.

`fpf.competency.define-done`
: The learner can phrase completion as a checkable state, not as effort spent.

`fpf.competency.handle-evidence`
: The learner can separate observation, hypothesis, decision, and future prediction.

`fpf.competency.write-decision-memory`
: The learner can preserve why a choice was made, what must remain true, and what will be checked
  later.

## Goldilocks Boundary

The first boundary is "can apply the move to a current task". The stretch is "can choose which move
matters now and avoid turning the method into ceremony".
