# Haircut Appointment

## Situation

You need to book a haircut, describe a simple preference, and stay in control if the hairdresser asks
something quickly.

## Working Script

```text
Je voudrais prendre rendez-vous pour une coupe.
C'est possible demain apres-midi ?
Un peu plus court sur les cotes, mais pas trop court dessus.
Vous pouvez repeter ?
```

## What This Tests

- Can the learner request an appointment without literal translation?
- Can they use time as a slot?
- Can they express degree and boundary?
- Can they repair misunderstanding instead of pretending to understand?

## Transfer

Replace `une coupe` with another service and keep the same structure:

- `pour une consultation`;
- `pour une table`;
- `pour une reparation`;
- `pour aujourd'hui`;
- `a 17h`.
